# InfraRisk AI Dashboard

AI-Powered Infrastructure Credit Risk & Financial Analytics Platform

## Overview

InfraRisk AI is an end-to-end risk analytics platform designed for infrastructure investment assessment. It combines Machine Learning, ESG Analytics, Climate Risk Monitoring, SHAP Explainability, Monte Carlo Simulation, Scenario Analysis, and Portfolio Ranking to support data-driven investment decisions.

## Key Features

### Credit Risk Analytics
- Infrastructure Credit Risk Prediction
- Default Probability Estimation
- Multi-factor Risk Scoring
- Financial Risk Fusion

### Explainable AI
- SHAP Feature Importance Analysis
- AI-driven Risk Explanation Engine
- Primary Risk Driver Detection

### ESG Risk Assessment
- Environmental Risk Scoring
- Social Risk Scoring
- Governance Risk Scoring
- Combined ESG Risk Index

### Climate Risk Monitoring
- Region-wise Climate Risk Analysis
- Flood Risk Assessment
- Heatwave Exposure Analysis
- Carbon Risk Assessment
- Climate Trend Visualization

### Advanced Risk Analytics
- Monte Carlo Risk Simulation
- Scenario Stress Testing
- Infrastructure Dependency Network (GNN Inspired)
- Portfolio Risk Heatmap

### Investment Intelligence
- Investment Recommendation Engine
- Portfolio Ranking System
- Top Asset Identification
- Executive Summary Generation

### Reporting
- Automated PDF Risk Reports
- Executive Summary Reports
- Risk Recommendation Reports

### Macroeconomic Analytics
- World Bank API Integration
- GDP Growth Monitoring
- Inflation Trend Analysis
